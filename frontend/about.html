About Battire

Welcome to Battire, your premier destination for cutting-edge electric vehicles. Our mission is to revolutionize transportation by providing eco-friendly, high-performance electric bikes that cater to the needs of modern commuters.

At Battire, we are committed to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Our team of experts is dedicated to designing and manufacturing electric vehicles that not only reduce carbon footprints but also offer a thrilling riding experience.

We proudly operate in 21 states with a robust network of 400 outlets

Our scooters are designed and developed in India with over 20 Design Patents Received.
